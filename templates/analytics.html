🧪 Input Features Description

🟢
Nitrogen (N)
Ratio of Nitrogen content in soil (kg/ha). Essential for vegetative growth and chlorophyll production.
🔵
Phosphorous (P)
Ratio of Phosphorous content in soil (kg/ha). Critical for root development and energy transfer.
🟡
Potassium (K)
Ratio of Potassium content in soil (kg/ha). Improves disease resistance and crop quality.
🌡️
Temperature
Average temperature of the region in °C. Affects metabolic rates and crop development stages.
💧
Humidity
Relative humidity of the region (%). Influences transpiration, disease pressure, and water stress.
⚗️
Soil pH
pH value of the soil (0-14). Controls nutrient availability and microbial activity in soil.
🌧️
Rainfall
Average rainfall in the region (mm). Determines water availability and irrigation requirements for crop production.
